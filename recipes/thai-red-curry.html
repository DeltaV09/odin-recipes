Thai Red Curry

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 Tbs coconut oil
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 leek
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 Tbs red curry paste
  • 1 can full-fat coconut milk (14 ounces)
  • 3 Tbs fresh cilantro
  • prepared brown rice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Melt coconut oil in large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and sear on both sides. Transfer to plate
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add bell pepper, leek, garlic, and ginger. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in curry paste. Cook for 5 minutes. Slowly pout in coconut milk, stirring to combine. Return chicken to skillet.
  5. Place skillet in the over and bake for 25 minutes. Chicken is fully cooked when thermometer reads 165F.
  6. Top with cilantro. Serve with rice.
