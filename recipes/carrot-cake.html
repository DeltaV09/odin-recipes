Carrot Cake

Two- or Three-layer carrot cake with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

Cake

  • Brown Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Eggs
  • Applesauce
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Ground Ginger
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Ground Cloves
  • Grated Carrots

Frosting

  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350, grease 2 or 3 9" cake pans.
  2. Whisk brown sugar, granulated sugar, oil, eggs, applesauce, vanilla.
  3. In separate bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and spices
  4. Power wet ingredients into dry, fold until just combined. Fold in carrots
  5. Pour batter evenly into cake pans. Bake 20-24 minutes for 3 pans, 30-35 minutes for 2 pans
  6. Make frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth
  7. Add powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt, Beat on high speed for 3 minutes
  8. Assemble and frost: Place one cake layer on plate, cover top with frosting. Add remaining cake layers, frosting in between. Spread remaining frosting on top and sides
